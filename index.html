Our Purpose

Why Prem
Education exists

Millions of children in India are denied the education they deserve — not because they lack potential, but because they lack access. We're here to change that.

We believe every child, regardless of where they are born or what they were born into, deserves the opportunity to learn, dream, and become.

Prem Education runs free learning centers, distributes school supplies, offers mentorship, and raises funds to support the most vulnerable students across underserved communities.

What we do

Programs built
with love

Our programs are designed by students, for students. Simple, direct, and proven to keep kids in school and learning.

Education

Free Tutoring Centers

Evening and weekend classes for students from low-income families. Volunteer teachers cover core subjects and help reduce dropout rates.
